JUST PUT THE SOCKS IN THE BOX... and we do the rest!
Our mission is simple. We intend to improve the health and mobility of those without a permanent address by providing them with clean dry socks.

IMPACT

Clean socks are consistently one of the top-requested items in homeless shelters. To combat a lack of comfortable and clean socks, we have donated thousands of socks to people in the WA community. Learn more below.
